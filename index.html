About


One of the big problems
with agriculture and wood industry is the effective surveillance of the property.
These normally cause problems due to the big area and difficult access. The current
solutions are either static, which can create blind spots and multiple undetected
entry points, or too expensive and not constant due to human nature.

Our company saw this as
an opportunity to provide our customers with a desperately needed alternative
that provides intruder and fire detection at a reasonable price, thus
preventing theft, arson, an wild fires.
